How It Works

The solver uses Cramer's rule to find the solution:

Example: 2x + 3y = 13 and x - y = -1

Enter: a₁=2, b₁=3, c₁=13, a₂=1, b₂=-1, c₂=-1

Solution: x = 2, y = 3
