Factorization Types

Quadratic: x² + 5x + 6 → (x + 2)(x + 3)

Difference of Squares: x² - 9 → (x + 3)(x - 3)

Perfect Square: x² + 6x + 9 → (x + 3)²

Common Factor: 2x² + 4x → 2x(x + 2)
